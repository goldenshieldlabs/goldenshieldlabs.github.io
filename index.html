About Us:

GoldenShield coin is a Government-free Currency for the Free Thought Era. By utilizing GoldenShield, or by contributing to the larger project and earning GoldenShield coins, you are making the world's first activist crypto-currency. GoldenShield runs using the IPFS network and the ethereum blockchain, creating a Permanent record with absolute no Centralized Authority insuring the Safety and Integrity of the Ecosystem. Every node in the network maintains verifiable storage of all transactions in the overall network. If one node goes down due to political interferance, that data remains safely stored in the rest of the entire network. All transactions remain anonymous, protecting the personal identification information for anyone contributing to the larger community. Support and facilitate the open access of information to the world's largest population. GoldenShield brings together the collective objective of activists seeking to end the systematic isolation of the people of North Korea, who have lived under government censorship online for a long time. Contributions to breaking algorithms and other network blockades will be provided a bounty. Participants agree to publicly verify any activity towards the larger goal through a communal system to be decided on later. Through the transactions and work produced from GoldenShield coin-holders, the larger community will pool global resources towards the larger goal.
